Books

My favorite book is called Exile. The book is about a twelve-year old girl, named Sophie Foster. She never seems to fit in anywhere even in a place where she is "supposed to belong." Despite her attempts to fit in she is never able to, but this may work to her advantage. Sophie must find a way to solve a problem before it destroys her entire world, including the people she loves. Once you start reading you will not be able to stop (until you get to the end!).

Movies

My favorite movie is called Maleficent. The movie is about the life of the villain from Sleeping Beauty. She was once the most loving, caring, fairy in the entire forest. But after an awful betrayal her heart turns to stone, and she is bent on revenge.

Some of my other favorite movies are all of the minion movies. I like these movies because I love minions especially BOB.
